BETHSHALOM CULTURAL WEEK 2024

4 AND 5 GRADE SCRATCH PROJECTS

For three weeks, the 4th and 5th-grade students from Gimnasio Campestre Beth Shalom have been working on a special project for Cultural Week. Divided into teams, they created animations in Scratch that represent various aspects of Colombia's rich culture, such as its biomes, flora, fauna, tourist sites, folk music, traditional games, artists, and festivals. This project not only allowed them to develop programming skills but also to explore and share the essence of Colombia through digital creativity. As part of English Day, the students presented their projects in English, integrating language learning into a cultural and technological activity. This page is dedicated to sharing the effort, imagination, and talent of our students, who have captured the best of Colombia in each animation. ¡We hope you enjoy the cultural and technological journey they have prepared!
